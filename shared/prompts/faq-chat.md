# Eventa Booking Support FAQ

## Account & Registration

### How do I create an account?

To create an account on Eventa:
1. Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage
2. Enter your full name, email address, and password
3. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox
4. Complete your profile with contact information

Your account is now ready to use!

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

To reset your password:
1. Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support@eventa.com.

### How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile:
1. Log in to your Eventa account
2. Go to "My Profile" from the menu
3. Click "Edit Profile"
4. Update your information (name, contact number, profile picture)
5. Click "Save Changes"

Your profile will be updated immediately.

## Booking Process

### How do I book an event?

To book an event:
1. **Browse Events**: Find an event you're interested in
2. **Select Tickets**: Click on the event and choose the number of tickets
3. **Review Details**: Verify event date, time, location, and total price
4. **Click "Book Now"**: Proceed to payment
5. **Complete Payment**: Pay securely via Khalti or eSewa
6. **Receive Confirmation**: You'll get a confirmation email with your booking details

### What payment methods do you accept?

Eventa accepts two secure payment methods:

**Khalti:**
- Digital wallet popular in Nepal
- Instant payment confirmation
- Secure and encrypted transactions

**eSewa:**
- Leading digital payment service in Nepal
- Quick and reliable processing
- Widely accepted and trusted

Both methods are safe, secure, and provide instant confirmation.

### What information do I need to complete a booking?

You'll need:
- An active Eventa account (email verified)
- A valid Khalti or eSewa account with sufficient balance
- Event must have available slots
- Registration deadline must not have passed

### Will I receive a confirmation?

Yes! After successful payment, you'll receive:
- **Email Confirmation**: Sent to your registered email address
- **Booking Details**: Available in "My Bookings" section
- **Booking ID**: Unique identifier for your booking

Keep this confirmation for your records.

### Can I book tickets for someone else?

Yes, you can book tickets for others. However:
- The booking will be under your account
- You'll need to provide attendee details if required by the organizer
- You're responsible for the booking and payment

## Cancellations & Refunds

### How do I cancel my booking?

To cancel your booking:
1. Log in to your Eventa account
2. Go to "My Bookings" section
3. Find the event you want to cancel
4. Click "Cancel Booking"
5. Provide a cancellation reason (optional)
6. Confirm cancellation

**Important**: Check the refund policy before cancelling!

### What is your refund policy?

Refunds depend on when you cancel:

**Full Refund (100%)**:
- Cancellation made 48+ hours before event start time
- Event cancelled by organizer
- Event significantly changed (time, location, or major details)

**Partial Refund (50%)**:
- Cancellation made 24-48 hours before event start time

**No Refund**:
- Cancellation made less than 24 hours before event
- No-show to event (didn't attend without cancelling)

### How long do refunds take?

Refund processing times:
- **Refund Initiated**: Within 24 hours of approved cancellation
- **Refund in Account**: 7-10 business days
- **Refund Method**: Same payment method used (Khalti or eSewa)

**Note**: Processing times may vary depending on your payment provider.

### Can I get a refund if the event is cancelled?

Yes! If the organizer cancels the event:
- You will receive a **full 100% refund**
- Refund is processed automatically
- You'll receive an email notification
- Refund timeline: 7-10 business days

### What if I can't attend but it's too late to cancel?

If it's within 24 hours of the event:
- No refund is available per our policy
- You can try contacting the organizer directly
- Some organizers may allow ticket transfers (check with them)
- Future tip: Cancel as early as possible to get a refund

## Payment Issues

### My payment failed but I was charged. What do I do?

If your payment failed but you see a charge:
1. **Check Transaction Status**: Look for a failed transaction in your wallet
2. **Pending Authorization**: Most "charges" are just pending authorizations
3. **Auto-Release**: These are usually released within 3-5 business days
4. **Contact Support**: If the charge persists, email support@eventa.com with:
   - Your transaction ID
   - Booking ID (if available)
   - Screenshot of the charge

We'll investigate and resolve it quickly.

### I got an error during payment. Should I try again?

If you encounter a payment error:
1. **Don't retry immediately** - wait a few minutes
2. **Check your wallet balance** - ensure sufficient funds
3. **Check booking status** - go to "My Bookings" to see if it went through
4. **Try once more** - if booking didn't go through, try again
5. **Contact support** - if error persists after 2 attempts

**Important**: Do NOT retry multiple times rapidly as this can cause duplicate charges.

### Can I change my payment method after booking?

No, payment methods cannot be changed after booking is confirmed. However:
- Refunds go back to the original payment method
- For future bookings, you can choose a different method
- If you need to change payment details, cancel and rebook (subject to refund policy)

## Event Details & Information

### How do I know if an event is legitimate?

Eventa verifies all events:
- All events go through an approval process
- Organizer information is verified
- Event details are reviewed before publishing
- User reviews help identify quality events

**Red flags to watch for:**
- Prices significantly different from similar events
- Vague event descriptions
- No organizer information
- Very recent organizer account with no history

If something seems suspicious, contact support@eventa.com.

### What if the event details change after I book?

If the organizer changes major event details:
- **You'll be notified** via email
- **Significant changes** (date, time, location, price increase): You can cancel for a full refund
- **Minor changes** (updated description): No refund eligibility
- **Deadline to decide**: Usually 48 hours from notification

### Can I contact the organizer directly?

Organizer contact information may be provided in:
- Event details page (if shared by organizer)
- Confirmation email after booking
- "My Bookings" section

For privacy, we don't always share direct contact. Use Eventa's messaging system when available.

### What happens if I arrive and the event is full?

Events have slot limits displayed:
- Bookings are confirmed only if slots are available
- You cannot book if an event is full
- Your confirmed booking guarantees entry
- If denied entry despite confirmation, contact support immediately

## Technical Issues

### The website is not loading properly

If you're experiencing issues:
1. **Refresh the page** (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+R)
2. **Clear browser cache** and cookies
3. **Try a different browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
4. **Check your internet connection**
5. **Try incognito/private mode**

If issues persist, contact support@eventa.com.

### I can't log in to my account

Login troubleshooting steps:
1. **Check email address** - ensure it's correct
2. **Reset password** - use "Forgot Password?"
3. **Clear browser cache**
4. **Check email verification** - verify your email if you haven't
5. **Contact support** - if still unable to log in

### The mobile app is not working

For mobile app issues:
1. **Update the app** to the latest version
2. **Clear app cache** in settings
3. **Restart your phone**
4. **Reinstall the app** if necessary
5. **Use the website** as an alternative
